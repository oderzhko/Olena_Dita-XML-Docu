How to save your PowerPoint template

In Microsoft PowerPoint you can use default templates for your presentations or you can create your own.

Here you will find description on how to save your PowerPoint template after creating it.
  1. In your PowerPoint, go to File >Save as, and select Browse.
  2. In the Save as dialog, give a name to your template and select PowerPoint Template as a file type. Your PowerPoint template will be saved in the Custom Office Templates folder.
Your PowerPoint template is added to the folder of custom templates.
