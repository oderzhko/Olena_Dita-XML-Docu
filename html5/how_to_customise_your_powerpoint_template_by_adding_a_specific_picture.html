How to customise your PowerPoint template by adding a specific picture

In Microsoft PowerPoint you can use default templates for your presentations or you can create your own.

Here you will find description on how to add a specific picture to your PowerPoint template.
  1. In your PowerPoint, go to File > New , click Blank Presentation.
  2. Once the new presentaion is open, go to Design tab > Themes and select a theme you like.
  3. Go to Format Background subtab and select Fill .
  4. Select Picture or Texture fill roundbox.
  5. Click Insert button to insert your picture from the local folder.
  6. Select the required picture and click the Insert button.
  7. In the Format Background tab, click the Apply to all button.
You have now created your very own PowerPoint template.
