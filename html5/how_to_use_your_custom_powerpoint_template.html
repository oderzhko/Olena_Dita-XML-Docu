How to use your custom PowerPoint template

Once your custom PowerPoint template is created, you can use it for your new presentation.

The steps below describe how to use your custom PowerPoint template for your presentations.
  1. In your PowerPoint, go to File > New .
  2. Select Custom subtab, click the Custom Office Templates folder.
  3. Select your custom PowerPoint template.
  4. Click the Create icon.
As a result your PowerPoint template is ready to be used for creating your presentation.
